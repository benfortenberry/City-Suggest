Kelly & Mignon Founders of Girl's Guide
Born out of a love of the city and a desire to share all of its wonders with the world, the Girl's Guide is the must have for all visitors to the Crescent City. The Girl's Guide is a complete Media resource including an app, a website, custom street maps, social media integration and so much more! As native New Orleanians, we are focused on promoting the best that the city has to offer while helping to support local businesses and events. Girls Guide Kelly & Mignon Founders of Girl's Guide
